Luzon


Name:Sisig
Description: Originating from Pampanga, this sizzling staple is made with chopped pig's face and ears, seasoned with calamansi, onions, and chili peppers, and usually served on a hot iron plate.
Name:Bagnet:
Description:A spicy, creamy delicacy made of taro leaves, chili peppers, and pork, all simmered slowly in rich coconut milk. Crispy, twice-cooked pork belly boiled in salted water until tender, then deep-fried until blistered and ultra-crunchy.

Description: A spicy, creamy delicacy made of taro leaves, chili peppers, and pork, all simmered slowly in rich coconut milk.





Visayas


Name:La Paz Batchoy
Description:is a famous Filipino noodle soup originating from the La Paz district of Iloilo City.It is made with fresh miki noodles, rich pork and beef broth, pork offal, and a raw egg, garnished with crushed chicharon, fried garlic, and spring onions.
Name:Cebu Lechon
Description:Cebu Lechon is a renowned Filipino roasted pig celebrated for its crackling, crispy skin and intensely flavorful, juicy meat.Often celebrated as the best in the country, this whole roasted pig is famous for its ultra-crispy skin and incredibly flavorful, herb-stuffed meat—requiring no dipping sauce.
Name:Bacolod Chicken Inasal
Description:is an iconic Filipino char-grilled chicken dish hailing from Bacolod City in the Visayas region.. It features chicken parts marinated in calamansi (Filipino lime), vinegar, garlic, ginger, and lemongrass, then basted with annatto (atsuete) oil while being grilled over hot coals to achieve a smoky, juicy finish.







Mindanao


Name:Pastil

Name:Tiyula Itum
Description:A signature Tausug dish often served at weddings and special occasions. A signature Tausug dish often served at weddings and special occasions. This savory, dark beef soup is deeply flavored with lemongrass, turmeric, and ground palapa (burnt coconut meat).
Name:Piyanggang Manok
Description: Originating from the Sulu archipelago.This flavorful Tausug recipe features chicken generously marinated and coated in a blackened coconut and spice paste, then grilled to perfection.
